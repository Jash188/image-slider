lion
Lions are majestic big cats known as the "king of the jungle," although they primarily inhabit savannas and grasslands in Africa, with a small population in the Gir Forest of India....
peacock
Peacocks, specifically the male of the peafowl species, are known for their stunning iridescent plumage and extravagant tail feathers, or "trains."
tiger
Tigers are the largest members of the big cat family and are renowned for their striking appearance and powerful presence.Each tiger's stripe pattern is unique, much like a human fingerprint.
horse
Horses are magnificent mammals known for their speed, strength, and graceful movement. They have played a vital role in human history, culture, and transportation for thousands of years.
elephant
Elephants are fascinating and intelligent animals, known for their large size, strong social bonds, and complex behaviors. There are three main species: the African bush elephant, the African forest elephant, and the Asian elephant.
